HELLO! Want to know your Akan name?

Well, this is a bit of how Akan-names are generated

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

Male Names

  • Sunday:Kwasi
  • Monday:Kwadwo
  • Tuesday:Kwabena
  • Wednesday:Kwaku
  • Thursday:Yaw
  • Friday:Kofi
  • Saturday:Kwame

Female Names

  • Sunday:Akosua
  • Monday:Adwoa
  • Tuesday:Abenaa
  • Wednesday:Akua
  • Thursday:Yaa
  • Friday:Afua
  • Saturday:Ama
